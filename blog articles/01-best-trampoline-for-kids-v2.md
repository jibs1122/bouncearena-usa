# Best Trampoline for Kids in 2026

Buying a trampoline for kids comes down to three things. Safety, build quality, and the right size for your yard.

The US market has a strong premium tier. Brands like Springfree, Acon, AlleyOOP, Vuly, and Jumpflex all build models that last well into the teen years.

Here are the ten best trampolines for kids in 2026, across price points from premium springless down to direct-to-consumer value.

## 1. Springfree Jumbo Round 13ft

[Springfree](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/springfree/) is the default safety pick for parents of younger kids. The composite rod system under the mat removes metal springs entirely, so there is no spring pinch hazard.

The Jumbo Round is the family-sized option in the lineup. It carries a 220 lb single-user weight limit, ASTM certification, and a 10-year warranty across the frame, mat, and net.

Price: $2,199.

## 2. Acon Air 14ft Round with Premium Net

[Acon](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/acon/) is a Finnish brand respected for bounce quality in the US market. The Air 14ft Round with Premium Net hits the family-yard sweet spot.

It uses 96 coil springs at 8.5 inches, a galvanized and powder-coated 2 inch steel frame, and is ASTM certified. The 14ft size suits kids of all ages and gives more usable mat space.

Price: $1,299.

## 3. Vuly Thunder 2 L (12ft)

[Vuly](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/vuly/) is an Australian brand with a growing US presence. The Thunder 2 is their flagship Leaf Spring model, using flat composite leaves hidden under the mat instead of traditional coils.

The L size sits at 145 inches in diameter with a 71 inch net height. Frame warranty is 10 years.

Price from $879.

## 4. AlleyOOP 14' Trampoline with Enclosure

[AlleyOOP](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/alleyoop/) is made by JumpSport, the company that pioneered the trampoline safety enclosure. The 14ft Round is their core family model.

It runs 96 springs, a 245 lb single-user limit, 2mm pre-galvanized steel walls, and a 10-year warranty on the mat, springs, and net. The zipperless entryway is a useful safety touch.

Price from $1,699.

## 5. AlleyOOP DoubleBounce 14'

The DoubleBounce is the pick if your kids are younger or just learning. AlleyOOP's patented two-mat system places a second tensioned layer under the main jumping surface, softening the initial impact of each bounce.

Same 14ft footprint as the standard model, with 128 springs.

Price from $2,249.

## 6. Jumpflex HERO 14ft

[Jumpflex](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/jumpflex/) sells direct to consumers, which keeps premium-tier specs at a value price. The HERO 14ft is the family pick in their lineup.

It uses 88 coil springs at 7.1 inches, carries ASTM certification, and has a 350 lb single-user limit with 550 lb combined capacity. Generous for households with multiple kids bouncing at once.

Price: $899.

## 7. Vuly Ultra 2 Pro L

The Ultra 2 Pro is Vuly's traditional coil-spring model, sitting below the Thunder 2 in price. The L size keeps the same 75 inch net height and 10-year frame warranty as the Thunder series.

A solid mid-tier pick if you want Vuly build quality without the Leaf Spring premium.

Price from $639.

## 8. Avyna Pro-Line 14' Round Above-Ground

[Avyna](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/avyna/) is a Dutch brand that has been making trampolines since 1999. The 14ft Round Above-Ground sits in the same premium tier as Springfree.

It runs 96 coil springs, an 11-gauge heavy steel frame, a lifetime frame warranty, and 10 years on the springs. Tested combined weight capacity is over 1,300 lb.

Price: $2,245.

## 9. Springfree Medium Round 10ft

The Medium Round is the smaller-yard option in the Springfree lineup. Same composite rod system, FlexiNet enclosure, and 10-year warranty as the Jumbo.

A 10ft footprint fits most suburban backyards with at least 3 feet of clearance on all sides. The 220 lb single-user limit stays the same.

Price: $1,649.

## 10. Acon Air 12ft Round with Premium Net

The 12ft Air is Acon's smaller family option. Same Air series spring system as the 14ft, just scaled down.

You get 8.5 inch springs, the galvanized powder-coated steel frame, and ASTM certification. The 12ft size suits one or two kids comfortably.

Price: $1,099.
