# Best Trampoline for Adults in 2026

Adults need a different trampoline than kids. Higher weight capacity, stronger frames, and rectangle shapes all matter more once you cross 200 lb.

Most adult buyers also want a trampoline that holds up to harder bouncing without warping or losing tension. The list below sticks to brands with adult-rated weight limits and proven build quality.

Here are the ten best trampolines for adults in 2026.

## 1. Acon HD 16 Rectangle (10x17ft)

The HD 16 is [Acon's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/acon/) performance rectangle and the strongest adult pick on the market. 140 coil springs, 2 inch galvanized and powder-coated steel frame, ASTM certified.

The 10x17ft footprint gives serious jumping room and the rectangular shape produces a more uniform bounce than rounds. Warranty is 10 years on the frame, 5 years on the mat and springs.

Price: $2,499.

## 2. AlleyOOP 10x17 Rectangle

[AlleyOOP's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/alleyoop/) rectangle is the premium spring competitor to the Acon HD 16. It uses 108 springs, a 280 lb single-user limit, and 2.5mm pre-galvanized steel walls.

Made by JumpSport and sold through specialty dealers. The 10-year warranty covers mat, springs, and net.

Price from $2,099.

## 3. Akrobat Primus Challenger 14x10

[Akrobat](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/akrobat/) is a Slovenian brand built around bounce performance. The Primus Challenger 14x10 carries a 331 lb single-user limit, 104 springs at 8.5 inches, and a 12-year spring warranty.

The bounce is the selling point. Akrobat markets up to 30% more rebound than standard backyard models.

Price: $3,099.

## 4. Acon Air 15ft Round with Premium Net

The 15ft Air is the largest round in Acon's lineup and the best round-shape option for adults. 110 coil springs at 8.5 inches, 35 inch mat height, ASTM certified.

If you want Acon bounce quality but prefer a round footprint over a rectangle, this is the pick.

Price: $1,499.

## 5. Jumpflex MEGA 17ft Rectangle

The MEGA is the rectangular flagship in [Jumpflex's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/jumpflex/) range. 98 springs at 9.8 inches, 500 lb single-user limit, 1000 lb combined capacity.

It is also the best direct-to-consumer value at the rectangle size, undercutting the Acon HD 16 and AlleyOOP 10x17 by several hundred dollars.

Price: $1,899.

## 6. Beast K9 10x17 Performance Rectangle

The K9 is [Beast's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/beast/) flagship performance trampoline. 124 springs at 9.5 inches, galvanized steel frame inside and out, marketed by the brand with no stated weight limit.

Positioned for tricks and athletic use. Solid pick for adults who want serious bounce without crossing into Acon or Akrobat pricing.

Price: $1,998.

## 7. Vuly Thunder Pro L (12ft)

The Thunder Pro is the top of [Vuly's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/vuly/) Leaf Spring lineup. The L size sits at 12ft diameter with a 75 inch net height and a 32 inch mat height.

The Leaf Spring system uses flat composite leaves under the mat, giving a deep bounce without traditional metal coil springs. 10-year frame warranty.

Price from $1,299.

## 8. Avyna 10x17 Rectangle Pro-Line Above-Ground

[Avyna's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/avyna/) 10x17 Rectangle is the European premium option. 128 coil springs, 11-gauge heavy steel frame, lifetime frame warranty, 10 years on the springs.

Tested combined weight capacity is over 1,300 lb. Pricing reflects the premium positioning.

Price: $3,745.

## 9. Jumpflex HERO 15ft Round

The HERO 15ft is the largest round in the Jumpflex lineup and the best DTC value at this size. 96 springs at 7.1 inches, 350 lb single-user limit, 550 lb combined capacity.

A strong pick for adults who want a round shape and a price under $1,000.

Price: $999.

## 10. AlleyOOP DoubleBounce 14'

The DoubleBounce is the adult pick for joint protection. AlleyOOP's patented two-mat system places a second tensioned layer under the main jumping surface, softening the initial impact stage of each bounce.

Worth considering if you have knees or a back that does not love hard landings. 128 springs, 14ft round footprint.

Price from $2,249.
