# Best Trampoline for Toddlers in 2026

Toddlers need a different kind of trampoline. Lower weight limits, handle bars, and shorter fall heights matter more than bounce performance.

The two main categories are indoor mini trampolines with grab rails (best for ages 1 to 3) and small outdoor models with safety nets (better for ages 3 to 5).

Here are the ten best trampolines for toddlers in 2026, across both categories.

## 1. Springfree Mini Round 6ft

[Springfree's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/springfree/) smallest model and the premium pick if you want a toddler-safe trampoline that grows into a full childhood option. The composite rod system under the mat replaces metal springs entirely, so there is nothing for small fingers to get caught in.

Carries a 175 lb single-user limit and a 10-year warranty across all parts. Around $849.

## 2. Plum Junior 7ft Trampoline with Safety Enclosure

Plum is a UK brand exclusive to Walmart in the US and aimed squarely at the young child segment. The 7ft Junior uses Plum's Springsafe design, which weaves the mat and pads together to keep small hands away from the springs.

The lower frame height makes it easier for toddlers to climb on and off. Around $259.

## 3. Little Tikes 3-Foot Trampoline

The default indoor mini for ages 3 and up. Plastic handlebar, 36 inch diameter, 55 lb weight limit.

A reliable starting point if you have not bought a toddler trampoline before. Around $80.

## 4. Galt Folding Trampoline

Galt has been making children's toys for over 180 years and the folding trampoline is its long-running toddler bestseller. Bungee cords replace metal springs, and the padded cover prevents little feet from slipping through.

77 lb weight limit, ages 3 and up, suitable for indoor and outdoor use. Around $130.

## 5. BCAN BT1 36in Toddler Foldable Bungee Trampoline

[BCAN's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/bcan/) toddler bungee model folds flat for storage and uses bungee cord tension instead of springs. Suitable for ages 3 and up with a 99 lb weight limit.

Around $150.

## 6. Fisher-Price WonderJump 3ft

Fisher-Price brings the trust factor for first-time toddler buyers. The WonderJump is a 36 inch indoor trampoline with a padded stabilizing handlebar designed for ages 3 and up.

A strong default if you already buy other Fisher-Price toys for the same child. Around $50.

## 7. Galt Nursery Trampoline

The Galt Nursery Trampoline is one of the few options designed specifically for ages 12 months to 3 years, which is younger than most toddler trampolines. The tortoise theme, 23 inch height, and easy-grip handle are all built around the very early walking and bouncing stage.

44 lb maximum weight. Around $60.

## 8. Plum Play 4.5ft Interactive Junior

The 4.5ft Interactive Junior adds LED lights around the rim that respond to bouncing, paired with Plum's standard low frame and full enclosure. Useful if your toddler responds well to sensory feedback or you want the trampoline to hold attention longer than a basic mini.

Designed for ages 3 and up. Around $200.

## 9. Little Tikes 7ft Trampoline with Safety Net Enclosure

The natural step up from a 36 inch indoor mini once your toddler outgrows it. The full safety net enclosure makes it suitable for outdoor use, and the 7ft footprint fits most yards.

Designed for ages 3 to 6. Around $312.

## 10. Jumpflex FLEX 8ft

[Jumpflex's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/jumpflex/) smallest model and the premium outdoor pick for older toddlers approaching 4 or 5. ASTM certified, galvanized steel frame, 330 lb combined weight rating.

A solid choice if you want a small backyard trampoline that will last well past the toddler years. Around $499.
