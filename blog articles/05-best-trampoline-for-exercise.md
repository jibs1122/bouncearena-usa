# Best Trampoline for Exercise in 2026

Most people searching for a fitness trampoline are looking for an indoor mini rebounder, not a backyard model. These are different products with different brand universes.

Rebounders use bungee cords or short springs to give a low-impact bounce designed for cardio, lymphatic drainage, and general fitness training. Most fold flat for storage and run quietly enough for apartment use. Quality ranges from $70 Amazon-tier minis to German-engineered $1,000 premium units.

Here are the ten best fitness trampolines for exercise in 2026.

## 1. Bellicon Premium 44"

Bellicon is the premium benchmark in the rebounder category. German-engineered, custom-assembled to the buyer's weight and bounce preference, with bungee strengths from Soft to Ultra Strong.

The 44" Premium supports up to 440 lb and is the size most fitness reviewers recommend over the smaller 39". Around $899 to $1,099 depending on options.

## 2. JumpSport 570 Pro Fitness Trampoline

JumpSport is the parent company of [AlleyOOP](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/alleyoop/) and runs a separate fitness rebounder line. The 570 Pro is the top of that range.

39" jumping surface, 325 lb weight capacity, elastic cord suspension. Around $599.

## 3. Cellerciser Half Fold

Cellerciser is built around lymphatic drainage marketing and has a strong following in the wellness and rebounder community. The Half Fold version stores easily and runs quieter than most spring-based rebounders.

40" diameter, three-spring suspension system. Around $549.

## 4. JumpSport 350 Pro

The 350 Pro is the value pick in the JumpSport range. Same elastic cord suspension as the 570, smaller frame and lighter build to keep the price down.

39" jumping surface, 250 lb weight capacity. Around $329.

## 5. Needak Soft Bounce

Needak is one of the few US-made rebounder brands and has built a reputation in the rebounding community for spring quality and frame durability. The Soft Bounce model uses softer springs for a deeper, lower-impact bounce.

40" diameter, foldable frame. Around $429.

## 6. BCAN BT4 Soft Land Pro 48"

[BCAN's](https://www.bouncearenareviews.com/brands/bcan/) flagship adult bungee model and the best value in the premium-feel tier. 500 lb single-user rating, adjustable bungee tension, 48" round frame with handle.

A solid alternative if a Bellicon is out of budget but a $100 Amazon mini feels too cheap. From $356.

## 7. JumpSport 220 Fitness Trampoline

The entry model in the JumpSport fitness range. Same elastic cord suspension as the 350 and 570, smaller frame and lower weight rating.

35" jumping surface, 200 lb weight capacity. Around $199.

## 8. Stamina 36" Folding Trampoline

Stamina is a long-running US fitness brand and the InTone Plus is one of the most widely owned entry-level rebounders in the country. Spring-based, foldable, basic.

36" diameter, around 250 lb capacity. Around $90.

## 9. BCAN BT2 Adult Foldable 40" Bungee

The smaller, lower-priced sibling to the BT4. Same bungee design, smaller jumping surface, foldable frame.

40" diameter, 450 lb single-user rating. Around $125.

## 10. Leaps & Rebounds Bungee Rebounder

A US-based rebounder maker that runs a 30-day money-back trial, similar to Bellicon's. Bungee suspension, customizable bungee strength based on weight.

40" diameter, around 350 lb capacity. Around $339.
