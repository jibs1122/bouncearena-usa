# ACON vs Beast

ACON and Beast both appeal to buyers who care more about bounce quality than bargain pricing, but they come from different parts of the market.

ACON is the more established premium backyard option, with a broader lineup and stronger warranty story. Beast is narrower and more trick-oriented, so the comparison is less about choosing between similar family trampolines and more about deciding how specialized you want the setup to be.

## Full Spec Comparison

Key takeaways

- Single-user weight isn't directly comparable: ACON (no published single-user limit; ~300 lb recommended on most models, no limit on high-end models); Beast (250 lb).
- ACON leads on warranty (frame 10 yr, mat 5 yr, springs 5 yr).
- Installation differs: ACON is mainly above-ground with an in-ground option, while Beast is above-ground only.
- Best-for: ACON reads as a premium performance spring brand; Beast as a high-end / trick-tramp style brand.
