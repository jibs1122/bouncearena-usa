# Vuly vs Akrobat

Vuly and Akrobat both sit above ordinary retail trampolines, but they are built around different buying priorities.

Vuly has the broader family ecosystem, with accessories and both coil-spring and leaf-spring models. Akrobat is more about European performance engineering, airflow, and in-ground capability.

If you are choosing between them, decide first whether you want a versatile family setup or a more specialized performance/in-ground trampoline.

## Full Spec Comparison

Key takeaways

- The two brands differ on bounce system — Vuly uses a mix of coil-spring and leaf-spring models, while Akrobat uses high-performance coil springs.
- Vuly offers round; Akrobat covers rectangle.
- If you want a very large trampoline, Vuly goes up to 16 ft round; Akrobat tops out at 12×17 ft rectangle.
- Akrobat leads on warranty (frame lifetime, springs 12 yr, net 5 yr).
- Installation differs: Vuly is above-ground only, while Akrobat is above- or in-ground.
