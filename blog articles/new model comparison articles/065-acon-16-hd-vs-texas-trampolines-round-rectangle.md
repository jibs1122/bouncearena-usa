# ACON 16 HD vs Texas Trampolines Round / Rectangle

Both are heavy-duty coil brands, so the split is weight limit and warranty. The ACON 16 HD recommends up to 330 lb per single-user at about $2,499, with a 10-year frame, 5 years on the mat and springs and 2 years on pads.

## Quick verdict

The 16 HD has the better-documented component warranty across the mat, springs and pads; Texas offers a higher weight limit and a lifetime frame.
